What's VEX?

VEX Robotics is the largest and fastest-growing robotics program for middle and high school students worldwide. VEX competitions combine competitive robotics with educational initiatives, encouraging students to apply Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) principles in a hands-on, real-world context.

Through VEX, students design, build, and program robots to compete in exciting tournaments that combine the tactical decision-making of a sport with the technical rigor of engineering. Each season presents a new game challenge that tests teams' creativity, problem-solving skills, and ability to work collaboratively under pressure.
