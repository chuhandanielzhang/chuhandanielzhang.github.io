RoboMaster RMUL - Kongfu Team

GTIIT, 2024-2025 | Mechanical Advisor & Co-Founder

RoboMaster is a robotics competition organized by DJI. University teams design, build, and program robots to compete in 5v5 matches.

I co-founded GTIIT's first RoboMaster team (Kongfu Team) and served as Mechanical Advisor and Co-Founder.

My Role: Mechanical Advisor & Co-Founder

  • Mechanical Design & FEA: Led mechanical design and used FEA to improve chassis strength and gimbal stability.
  • Chassis & Mobility: Designed a holonomic X-drive with a circular chassis for fast spinning maneuvers.
  • Suspension: Designed independent suspension for stable traction.
  • Integration: Built a stiff gimbal structure and coordinated mechanical assembly.
