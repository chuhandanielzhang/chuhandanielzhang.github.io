Rebound Robotics - Basketball Training Robot

Design for Manufacturing, GTIIT, 2024 | Team Lead & CAD Designer

Course: Design for Manufacturing (034371), Dr. Anand Prakash Dwivedi

Led a team to design a basketball training robot with navigation, ball collection, and passing functions.

My Role: Lead CAD Designer & FEA Engineer

Mechanical System Design

  • Omnidirectional Chassis: Four mecanum wheels for movement in any direction.
  • Collection System: Elastic rollers with a motor-driven conveyor.
  • Internal Hopper: Holds up to 6 balls.
  • Dual Friction-Wheel Launcher: PWM speed control for adjustable pass speed.
